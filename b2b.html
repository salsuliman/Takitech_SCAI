Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pressing Intensity?
Pressing Intensity is our proprietary metric that measures the effectiveness of a team's pressing actions. It combines player positioning, movement, speed, and tactical coordination to provide a comprehensive analysis of defensive pressure.
How is the AI analysis generated?
Our AI system analyzes tracking data, event data, and historical patterns to generate tactical insights. It identifies pressing patterns, effectiveness, and provides recommendations for improvement based on advanced algorithms and models.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect in the next billing cycle, and pricing will be adjusted accordingly.
Do you offer custom solutions for specific needs?
Yes, our Enterprise plan includes custom solutions tailored to your organization's specific requirements. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs and we'll create a package that works for you.
Which leagues do you cover?
Our Basic plan covers the top 5 European leagues, while the Pro plan extends to the top 10 global leagues. Enterprise clients can request coverage for additional leagues or competitions.
How often is new data added?
Match data is typically processed and made available within 24 hours after the game concludes. Pro and Enterprise subscribers get priority access to new data as soon as it's available.
